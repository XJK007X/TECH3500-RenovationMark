Our Story

Coming of the end of the Covid crisis and lockdown the carpenter company our owner worked for tried to pull a little trick reseting the contracts of all its employees. Seeing this our owner Marek declined the rehire and decided to use his connections aquired over the years and his experience to start up his own renovation and construction service for homes and apartments.

  • NO payment needed till work is finished, all shopping done by us plus delivery and transport
